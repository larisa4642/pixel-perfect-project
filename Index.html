About the GTnetwork

The GTnetwork is a free, innovative, and easy to use platform for healthcare professionals who want to build their knowledge of gene therapy. Driven and reviewed by an internationally renowned steering committee, it host media-rich educational resources developed by AveXis, Inc.

The GTnetwork is a place where you can:

  • Access up-to-date, clinically relevant gene therapy information, including the latest news at your fingertips
  • Explore an ever-expanding gene therapy resource library
  • Interact with your peers to share information within a closed environment
